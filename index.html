01MethodAbout the Voice — Naturals-trained
Fig. 02 · The Materials
Naturals-trained Enfleurage, NYC Expo regular Replies to every comment

An investigation into the intention behind the bottle.

I got my start at Enfleurage, an aromatherapy shop in New York City. Working there gave me a foundation in naturals that most people never get — raw materials, absolutes, the building blocks of everything we smell. That education shaped how I evaluate fragrance to this day.

When I smell something, I'm looking for the intention behind it. Every perfume was made with a purpose — by a specific perfumer, with specific ingredients, for a specific reason. I want to dig into that. There's always more to discover behind the bottle than what it sprays.

Fragrance needs to be described in a way that transports you — not just catalogued.

I end every video the same way: stay fabulous, sample first. Before I go, I try to leave you with a visual — something that puts you somewhere. The fragrance community has real camaraderie around a shared obsession. I travel to expos for it — different genres, cultures, traditions.

An independent, a niche, a mass-market fragrance — I investigate all of them with the same integrity. If you take time to watch me, I take time to respond to you. Every comment.
